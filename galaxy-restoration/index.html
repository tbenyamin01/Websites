Do you inspect the roof before quoting?

Yes, always. We take the time to properly inspect your roof so the quote reflects what your roof actually needs, nothing more, nothing less. Call to arrange a free inspection.

How do I know if my roof needs restoring or just repairing?

Cracked or slipped tiles, crumbling ridge capping, faded or mossy tiles and leaks after rain are the usual signs. Sometimes a repair is all you need, and we will tell you honestly which one it is. Call and describe what you are seeing.

Can you fix a leaking roof?

Yes. We track down the real source of the leak, which is often not where the water shows up inside, and fix it properly so it does not come back. If your roof is leaking, call us sooner rather than later, water damage only gets more expensive.

How long does a roof restoration take?

It depends on the size and condition of your roof and the weather. We work carefully and do the job properly rather than rushing it. Call 0425 343 643 and we will give you a realistic timeframe for your roof.
