What we do

Mobile dog grooming, brought to your door

Everything your dog needs to look, feel and smell amazing, without leaving home.

Full Wash & Blow Dry

A thorough warm wash with quality shampoos matched to your dog's coat, finished with a fluffy blow dry. Your pup comes out soft, clean and smelling beautiful.

Grooming & Coat Tidy

Trimming and tidying to keep your dog neat, comfortable and cool. We work at your dog's pace so the whole experience stays calm and positive.

Deshedding Treatment

Perfect for dogs that leave fur all over the house. Specialist shampoos and tools remove the loose undercoat so there is far less hair on your floors and furniture.

Nail Trimming & Paw Care

Careful nail trims that keep your dog walking comfortably and protect your floors. Done gently, even for dogs who hate having their paws touched.

Ear Cleaning & Hygiene

Gentle ear cleaning and hygiene checks as part of the groom. Little details that keep your dog healthy and comfortable between visits.

Puppy First Groom

A calm, positive first grooming experience that sets your puppy up for life. Lots of patience, gentle handling and plenty of reassurance.
